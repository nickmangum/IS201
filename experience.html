Bright Planet Solar, Inc.

Utilities Assistant Manager (Currently)

July 2021 - Current

Throughout my time at Bright Planet Solar, I started out as a regular Interconnection Coordinator. With time and effort, I've rose up in the ranks to an Area Lead, Supervisor, Process Supervisor, and I am now an Assistant Manager in the Utilities Department with around twenty of my own employees, four of which are Supervisors.

I oversee the interconnection of customers' installed solar panels to their respective utility company's electrical grids in the East Coast area (including Connecticut, Massachussetts, Illinois, New Jersey, New York, New Hampshire) and also Puerto Rico.

This career consists of an incredible amount of problem solving, management, communication, and coding. I use Microsoft Excel Visual Basic for Applications on a daily basis in creating my own reports to monitor the productivity and progress of my agents while innovating new ways to improve our processes in our Utilities Department. Bright Planet Solar is a relatively new company and due to that fact, there is much work to be done in improving the processes of our teams.
